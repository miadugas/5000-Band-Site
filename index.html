Hi, It’s Meghann!

Based in Denver, CO, 5000 plays original music as a duo and a four piece band. Started by Meghann Jordan & Matt Youngblood. The current 4-piece line up for 5000 includes drummer Koby Chapman and bassist Mia Dugas.
Original music via a Minneapolis-meets-Athens alterna-punk with strong 90s influence, particularly Dinosaur Jr., R.E.M. and Tom Petty'ish influence.

Meghann (5000) Jordan came up in the Athens, GA music scene. Surrounded by such influences as R.E.M. and Vic Chesnutt, and other local musicians, she started playing guitar at age 13 and writing songs at 18. Matt spent the 2000's in Minneapolis fronting his three-piece rock band "Chunky Jackson". They were heavily influenced by local bands such as Hüsker Dü and the Replacements. Meghann and Matt met through longtime musical cohorts Dave Schools and Jerry Joseph. Both moved to Denver & started an original rock band. They have been gigging as a duo (and sometimes a 4-piece rock band) for the past 3.5 years locally, as well as nationally.

In early 2017, Meghann returned to Athens to record her first 6-song EP under the name "5k" backed by Creston Spiers and Paul Trudeau of Harvey Milk with Mike Albanese from Espresso Machine Studios. In April 2017 Meghann and Matt played their first show at The Winter Park Pub in Winter Park, Colorado which led to some national dates. In February 2018, with their original 4-piece line up, they recorded their first single “Anger” with Kyle Jones at Sleeping Brotherhood Music Recording Studio in Denver, CO. They continued playing duo shows and a few full band shows in the summer of 2018 with an ever-evolving cast of players. In the interim, 5000 the Duo recorded a series of singles at KMG Life Studios in Boulder, CO and Shitshow Studios in Denver with Eric Martinez.

The current line up includes Meghann (vocals, guitar), Matt (lead guitar, vocals), Koby Chapman (drums & precussion) and Mia Dugas (Bass). Koby 'k.biz' Chapman was born and raised in Denver, CO. At the age of 12 he began to play the drums, taking lessons at his local drum shop and joining school bands. Koby's background includes hip hop and electronic music. Mia, originally from Louisiana, cut her teeth in the Atlanta music scene in the 90's playing throughout the SE US with Alterna/Psychedelic Jam-Band "touch" and has been grooving ever since. 5000 has been playing regularly around Colorado and the Denver metro-area and are currently at work on a full-length album with Todd Divel of Silo Sound set to release in early of 2020.
